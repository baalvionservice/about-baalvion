## Overview

**About Baalvion** is the corporate site that tells the story of the group — our mission to build
next-generation global B2B trade infrastructure, the businesses we operate, and the people behind
them.

## ✨ Features

- **Vision & mission** — who we are and what we are building
- **Businesses** — the platforms and ventures across the group
- **Leadership** — the team driving Baalvion forward
- **CMS-driven** — content managed centrally, delivered through a public API
